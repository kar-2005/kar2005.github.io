Common Questions

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about my services.

What services do I offer?

I offer a wide range of creative services including Brand Identity (Logo design, brand strategy), Digital Design (UI/UX, web design, social media assets), and Motion & Video (Motion graphics, video editing, promos).

How much does a branding project cost?

Pricing is tailored to the specific needs and scale of each project. I offer customized packages ranging from basic startup identity kits to comprehensive enterprise rebranding. Contact me for a personalized quote.

What is my typical project timeline?

Timeline varies by project scope. A logo project typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a full website or branding overhaul might take 4-8 weeks. I provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project.

Do I offer ongoing support or retainers?

Yes, I offer monthly retainer packages for clients who need continuous design support, social media content, or website maintenance. This ensures priority service and consistent brand quality.

Do I provide source files?

Yes! Upon project completion and final payment, I transfer all ownership rights and provide all necessary source files (AI, PSD, EPS, PDF, PNG, JPG) organized for your convenience.

How do we get started?

Simply fill out my contact form or send me an email. We'll schedule a discovery call to discuss your goals, requirements, and how I can help upgrade your brand.
